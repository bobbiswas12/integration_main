Technical Integration

The arena for analytical minds and future innovators

The Tech chapter hosts highly anticipated events like the Mathematics Talent Reward Programme (MTRP), a rigorous national-level math olympiad, and SPARQ, a data analytics contest in collaboration with ISI Kolkata's CAIML.
